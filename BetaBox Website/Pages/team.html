Our Story

Betabox was founded in 2013 by Mondelez Global and Prehype to solve industry-wide product sampling problems. The team is comprised of experts in brand marketing, sampling, logistics, social media, and mobile-consumer technology. During Betabox's closed beta, the team has already begun working with many of the world's most notable brands.
