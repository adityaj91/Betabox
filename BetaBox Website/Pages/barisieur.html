The Barisieur

An alarm clock and coffee brewer. It eases the user into the day with the subtle movement of stainless steel ballbearings that boil the water through induction heating, accompanied by the smell of freshly brewed coffee. It encourages a ritual before going to sleep, signalling to the body and mind that it is time to unwind and relax. Living slow even when times are fast.

    Features & Specs
  • Press the dispenser button to automatically dispense tape to any desired length
  • Release the button and the dispenser automatically cuts the tape
  • Can be used with one hand only
  • Ideal for home, office, store and warehouse use
  • Tape thickness is 0.05 - 0.07 mm and tape size is 19 mm (3/4") width x 32.9 meter (1,296") length
  • Works without batteries!
